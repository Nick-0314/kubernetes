## 从节点加入集群

刚刚主节点生成了token

然后执行kubeadm join 命令

```
kubeadm join 192.168.10.10:6443 --token 6ldwab.otdc2blhzzy9ee9a \
    --discovery-token-ca-cert-hash sha256:18c5ec903bed7e06fba0fcd81d1974d2652b992d3c7fea9875e895169a86717d 
```
